MEET THE BAND

From the first time Juliet and Ryan sat down to play together, they were writing songs that felt both familiar and new. Drawing on influences from Wilco to Sarah Jarosz to Slayer, the duo began writing songs with the poetic lyrics and haunting harmonies that make up the Good Luck Alaska sound.

While Good Luck Alaska came together only recently, Ryan and Juliet have played music together since 2016 in Twin Cities band Geyser. Good Luck Alaska is now working on their debut EP, expected to come out this Spring.
